## 🌟 About Phishtra

**Phishtra** is a state-of-the-art cybersecurity suite built to combat modern phishing, typosquatting, and brand impersonation attacks. By leveraging a high-performance, embedded **WebAssembly (WASM)** detection engine, Phishtra intercepts dangerous navigations *before* they can render in the browser.

Unlike traditional cloud-based antivirus software that transmits your browsing history to remote servers for analysis, **Phishtra performs 100% of its threat detection locally on your device.** 

What happens on your device stays on your device.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 🔒 100% Offline Analysis
No telemetry. No tracking. No API calls to remote servers containing your URLs. Phishtra's advanced heuristics and embedded rulesets run entirely within the sandbox of your browser or local Android environment.

### 🛑 Proactive Shield Interception
By the time a traditional scanner warns you about a malicious site, tracking pixels and malicious scripts may have already executed. Phishtra's Proactive Shield acts as a barricade, safely intercepting the navigation request and displaying a local warning screen *before* the target page ever loads.

### 📱 Cross-Platform Ecosystem
- **Browser Extension**: Seamlessly integrates into Chrome, Edge, and Safari to monitor web traffic in real-time.
- **Android App**: Utilizes deep OS integrations (Notification Listeners & Local VPN routing) to protect users across all their mobile apps, not just the browser.

### 📊 Deep Diagnostic Insights
When a threat is blocked, Phishtra doesn't just say "Blocked." It provides the user with transparent, actionable insights explaining *why* the URL was flagged (e.g., homograph attacks, untrusted top-level domains, embedded zero-width characters).

## 🛡️ Security & Privacy Stance

Phishtra treats privacy as a fundamental product requirement, not an optional feature. 
We operate under a strict zero-knowledge model:
1. **Zero Logging**: We do not log the sites you visit.
2. **Zero Transmission**: Your URLs are never sent to a cloud server.
3. **Zero Monetization**: We do not sell, share, or analyze your personal browsing data.
