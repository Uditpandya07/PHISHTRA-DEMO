## 🌟 About Phishtra
Phishtra is an advanced, offline-first, privacy-respecting anti-phishing solution designed for both Desktop (Browser Extension) and Mobile (Android App). 

Unlike traditional cloud-based solutions, Phishtra relies on a powerful local WebAssembly engine to instantly detect and block malicious links, brand impersonations, and typosquatting—all while ensuring your browsing history never leaves your device.

## 🚀 Features Highlights
- **100% Offline Analysis**: Keep your data safe locally without telemetry tracking.
- **Proactive Shield**: Blocks dangerous links before the web page can even load.
- **Cross-Platform**: Protection available as a Browser Extension and Android Notification Listener.
- **Privacy-First**: No data is sold, transferred, or monitored. What happens on your device stays on your device.
